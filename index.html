This is more than just entertainment!

Nordville Technology LTd is a privately owned company established in Malta. Our team consists of very professional and creative directors, artists and developers backed by a cutting-edge technology. The company starts focusing on creating sport simulators in 2018. It is also known for having worked on CRM integration and client server systems previously. In 2018 we’ve been reorganized to start developing realistic games and to publish free-to-play games for PC on the basis of our previous experience.

Our first product in this direction is the boxing simulator game “Cyber Fight Challenge” and now this is our main business area. We strived to create a real boxing contest ambiance, powered by the advanced technology of Unreal Engine, that allows us to quickly engage and retain our players. Our goal is to bring online gaming experience on an unprecedented scale with a full digital 3-D scenatios featuring a wide range of moves and punches. The game can be palyed as a two-players versus single-player game against the computer. Our highest industry standard lead us to develop the ultimate solutions and to provide unrivalled gaming services.
Within our strategy is also focusing on support of sport.

We contribute to maintenance and development of cybersports, i.e. boxing.

GAME DEVELOPMENT

We have developed a Cyber Fight Challenge which is a boxing simulator to be played on PC online. This game allows players to play together and to take part in multiple real-time competitions which makes the game more attractive and exciting. The game includes many levels suitable for players of different skills and experiences. The designs of our characters is based on human models who had their action shots taken and processed earlier by professional designing crews.

We strive to deliver superior graphics, to make our boxing a grasping game full of remarkable aesthetics and unrivalled gaming entertainment for those who wishes to try himself as a true boxer. We took the rules of a professional boxing as a basis. Combining the atmosphere of a boxing tournament with play dynamics makes it possible to deliver thrilling action sequences to our players.

We offer our players online boxing tournaments based on professional boxing rules. Thousands of Tournaments every day that get together our players on a competitive gaming ring and compete for amazing prizes. All you need is just register on our site, download the game and you are ready to start! 3 weights categories, several characters and 10 boxing ring are available 24/7. Up to 20 players compete in every tournament. . The score is based on player’s actions. Every event is a fun experience. The strongest one wins the prize!

Real ambiance of boxing tournament with a superior graphics that transfer players to captivating boxing universe.

Nothing is more exciting than live tournaments. Up to 10 boxing rings available for each tournament. Our tournaments will deliver a one of a kind gaming entertainment for all participants. Our events promise to our participants a genuine endorphin rush. Motion-graphic production goes in tandem with dynamic sound effects and this makes it possible to transfer our boxers to imaginative universe.
